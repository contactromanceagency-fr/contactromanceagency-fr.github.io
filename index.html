Schedule and publish your TikTok content in one place

Romance Content Studio is a content management tool that lets our team connect a TikTok account and publish or schedule short videos directly through the official TikTok Content Posting API.

Login with TikTok

You will be redirected to TikTok to authorize the connection. We only access the accounts you explicitly connect.

Connect securely

Sign in with TikTok using the official OAuth flow. You grant access to your own account and can revoke it anytime from TikTok settings.

Publish directly

Send a finished video straight to the connected account, with caption and privacy settings, using the Content Posting API.

Upload as draft

Prefer to review first? Send the video to your TikTok inbox as a draft and finish publishing it from the TikTok app.

How it works

  1. Click Login with TikTok and authorize the connection on TikTok.
  2. Select the video you want to share and add your caption.
  3. Choose to publish it directly or send it as a draft to your TikTok inbox.
